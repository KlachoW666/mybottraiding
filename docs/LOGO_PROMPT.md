# Промпт для логотипа CryptoSignal Pro

Стиль и цвета — как в нашем приложении: тёмный фон, зелёный акцент, минимализм, профессионализм. Название продукта: **CryptoSignal Pro**.

Используйте в Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion или передайте дизайнеру.

---

## Краткий промпт (в стиле приложения)

```
Logo for "CryptoSignal Pro" — cryptocurrency trading signals and market analysis app. Style: dark theme like our app — dark gray background (#242424), green accent (#57CA7A, #47A663). Icon: ascending signal line or chart breakout, minimal, flat. Font feel: Inter, clean sans-serif. Professional, recognizable at 32px. No coins, no rockets. Wordmark "CryptoSignal Pro" with "Pro" in accent green.
```

---

## Расширенный промпт (стиль приложения + название)

```
Create a logo for "CryptoSignal Pro" that matches our application style.

**Product name (use exactly):** CryptoSignal Pro  
**Product:** Cryptocurrency market analysis and trading signals (desktop + web). Dashboard, signals, OKX, charts.

**Visual style (from our app):**
- Dark base: #242424 (background), #303030 (cards) — as in our UI
- Accent: green #57CA7A, #47A663 — buttons, success, charts in our app
- Gradient accent: #3D8F55 → #62BC7C (like our topbar/brand block)
- Minimal, flat, no 3D. Rounded corners ~8px if needed (like our cards)
- Typography: Inter-style, clean, medium weight. "CryptoSignal" + "Pro" (Pro can be accent green or smaller)

**Icon idea (one strong mark):**
- Ascending signal/chart line that suggests "CryptoSignal" — growth, data, clarity
- Or: stylized "C" or "CSP" with a small signal/chart element
- Must work on dark (#242424) and on green accent. Also readable at 32×32 (favicon).

**Mood (as in our app):**
- Professional, serious, trustworthy
- Fintech, not playful. White/gray text on dark, green for actions and success
- Memorable and recognizable

**Deliverables:** Icon only (for app icon, favicon); optional: full wordmark "CryptoSignal Pro" in our style. Colors: dark gray + green accent only, matching our app palette.
```

## Палитра из нашего приложения (для логотипа)

| Назначение   | Цвет / переменная | HEX       |
|-------------|-------------------|-----------|
| Фон тёмный  | --bg-base         | #242424   |
| Карточки    | --bg-card-solid   | #303030   |
| Акцент      | --accent          | #57CA7A   |
| Акцент тём. | --accent-dark     | #47A663   |
| Градиент    | --accent-gradient | #3D8F55 → #62BC7C |
| Текст       | --text-primary    | #FFFFFF   |
| Текст вторичный | --text-secondary | rgba(255,255,255,0.7) |

Шрифт в приложении: **Inter** (Google Fonts).
